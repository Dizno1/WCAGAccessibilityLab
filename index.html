    Keyboard Shortcuts and Navigation

    Open Door Accessibility Lab is designed for both traditional screen reader browse mode and a faster keyboard-driven workflow. Choose the approach that works best for your testing and learning style.

    Fast Practice Mode - Virtual Cursor Off

    Press Ctrl+S to start a practice session from anywhere in the application. Press R to repeat the current question. Press A to hear the available answer choices. Press 1 through 4 to select an answer. Press C to continue to the next question after answering. Press N to skip the current question.

    Browse Mode - Virtual Cursor On

    Use heading navigation to move between major sections of the application. After a question is announced, move to the Answer Choices heading and continue reading or use form navigation to reach the answer buttons. After selecting an answer, focus moves to the Continue button.

    Recommended Learning Flow

    Beginner and Intermediate levels are intended to build recognition and familiarity with WCAG Success Criteria. Advanced and Expert levels introduce more realistic testing and audit scenarios designed to strengthen analysis, decision making, and accessibility evaluation skills.

    Review Lab

    After a session is complete, review missed questions, examine related W3C guidance, challenge answers that may have multiple valid interpretations, and export challenges for instructor review.
